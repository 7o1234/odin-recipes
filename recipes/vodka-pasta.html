Vodka Pasta

Description

This is a simple and easy pasta dish that anyone can make. It was made popular by model Gigi Hadid, but it does not take away from its flavor. Using simple ingredients that can be found in your house, there's almost no reason to not make this dish. However, we do advise to not use preshredded cheese, as the freshly shredded adds a new dimension to the flavor.

Ingredients

  • Kosher salt
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 4 oz Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 4.5 oz tomato paste
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flake
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 lb rigatoni

Instructions

  1. Boil water with salt in large pot
  2. Peel and finely chop onion
  3. Smash garlic and remove peel
  4. Grate Parmesan
  5. Heat oil in dutch oven on high, then add garlic and onion stirring constantly until edges turn brown, 5-7 min
  6. Add tomato paste and red pepper flakes, stirring often, 5-7 min
  7. Add vodka to pan to deglaze, scrape bottom, then reduce heat to low
  8. Take 1/4 cup of boiling water into measuring cup and add heavy cream
  9. Slowly add cream to dutch oven until sauce forms, then remove from heat
  10. Cook rigatoni in boiling water according to package instructions, making sure to keep 1 cup of pasta liquid in reserve
  11. Heat dutch oven on low and add rigatoni to sauce
  12. Add pasta liquid and half of Parmesan, then mix
  13. Season with salt to taste
  14. Divide and serve, optional basil leaves for garnish
